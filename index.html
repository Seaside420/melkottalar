Welcome to the open-access repository for research on the Indus script interpreted as the Mel-Kottalar maritime ledger. The site provides direct access to the principal publication, The Indus Script Trade Ledger (PDF), alongside key supporting documents from the field. Readers are invited to review the pattern analysis, etymological reconstructions, and proposed decipherment framework.
